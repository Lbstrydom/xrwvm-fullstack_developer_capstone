Real deployment on IBM Cloud / SN Labs Kubernetes:
- Django app image: us.icr.io/sn-labs-strydomlouis/dealership:latest (server/Dockerfile)
- Node/Express backend image: us.icr.io/sn-labs-strydomlouis/nodeapp:latest (server/database/Dockerfile)
- MongoDB deployed in-cluster (mongo:latest), Node app deployed in-cluster and wired
  to it via a "mongo-db" Service and the MONGO_HOST env var
- Django's backend_url points at the in-cluster "nodeapp-service" Service
- Exposed via Skills Network Toolbox (port 8000), backed by kubectl port-forward
  from the SN Labs Theia session
